About the Cognitive Liberty Foundation

Our mission is to defend and advance the fundamental right to freedom of thought, mental privacy, and self-determination in the digital age. We advocate for ethical technology, informed policy, and the protection of cognitive liberty for all.

Our Values

  • Freedom of Thought: Every person has the right to think freely and independently.
  • Mental Privacy: Personal thoughts and mental data must be protected from unauthorized access or manipulation.
  • Ethical Technology: We promote the responsible development and use of neurotechnology and AI.
  • Education & Advocacy: Empowering the public and policymakers with knowledge and resources.
  • Inclusivity: We believe cognitive liberty is a universal human right.
